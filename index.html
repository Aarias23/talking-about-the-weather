Weather Conversations

Teaching resource by Alexis Arias

Conversation 1

Speaker 1: Hi, it's Lisa. I'm in Los Angeles.
Speaker 2: Los Angeles? Why are you there?
Speaker 1: I'm here for a meeting.
Speaker 2: How's the weather? Is it sunny?
Speaker 1: Yes, it's very sunny.
Speaker 2: Is it hot?
Speaker 1: Yes, it's really hot. I should have brought sunscreen.
Speaker 3: What are you talking about?
Speaker 1: The weather in LA. It's very hot.

Conversation 2

Speaker 1: Hi, it's Emma. I'm in Phoenix.
Speaker 2: Phoenix? What are you doing there?
Speaker 1: I'm here for a conference.
Speaker 2: How's the weather? Is it sunny?
Speaker 1: Yes, it's very sunny and hot.
Speaker 2: Is it too hot?
Speaker 1: Yes, but I'm used to it.
Speaker 3: What's going on?
Speaker 1: We're talking about the weather in Phoenix. It's super hot.
Speaker 3: That sounds tough! It's cool and cloudy in Portland.

Conversation 3

Speaker 1: Hi, Mark. It's Sarah. I'm in Seattle.
Speaker 2: Seattle? Why are you there?
Speaker 1: I'm visiting friends.
Speaker 2: How's the weather? Is it sunny?
Speaker 1: No, it's raining.
Speaker 2: Is it cold?
Speaker 1: Yes, it's cold and wet.
Speaker 3: What are you talking about?
Speaker 1: The weather in Seattle. It's gray and rainy.
Speaker 3: That's typical for Seattle. It's sunny here in San Diego.

Conversation 4

Speaker 1: Hi, Mike. It's Alice. I'm in San Diego.
Speaker 2: San Diego? What are you doing there?
Speaker 1: I'm on vacation.
Speaker 2: How's the weather? Is it sunny?
Speaker 1: Yes, it's very sunny and warm.
Speaker 2: Is it too hot?
Speaker 1: No, it's perfect. I'm enjoying the beach.
Speaker 3: What are you talking about?
Speaker 1: The weather in San Diego. It's great here.
Speaker 3: That sounds amazing! It's cold in Chicago.

Conversation 5

Speaker 1: Hi, Emily. It's David. I'm in Chicago.
Speaker 2: Chicago? Why are you there?
Speaker 1: I'm here for work.
Speaker 2: How's the weather? Is it sunny?
Speaker 1: No, it's snowing.
Speaker 2: Is it cold?
Speaker 1: Yes, it's freezing cold. I miss the warmth.
Speaker 3: What are you discussing?
Speaker 1: The weather in Chicago. It's snowing and cold.
Speaker 3: Stay warm! It's sunny in Miami.

Conversation 6

Speaker 1: Hi, Tom. It's Rachel. I'm in New York.
Speaker 2: New York? What brings you there?
Speaker 1: I’m here visiting family.
Speaker 2: How’s the weather? Is it nice out?
Speaker 1: Not really; it’s cloudy and windy.
Speaker 2: Is it cold?
Speaker 1: Yes, pretty chilly. I wish I’d brought a warmer jacket.
Speaker 3: What are you all chatting about?
Speaker 1: The weather in New York. It's windy and cold today.
Speaker 3: I feel you! It’s warm and sunny here in Houston.
